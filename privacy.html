This Privacy Policy is meant to help you understand what information we collect, why we collect it and how you can delete your information.

Information Identi Collects

Our search product rides on several, multiple and diversified data sources. Identi does not usually collect user information directly.

  • Information Organizations Provide

User information including name, email, phone number, Bank Verification Number (BVN), and picture are provided by third-party organizations who’s Privacy Policies, we believe, allow for such.

  • Information Users Provide

Sometimes users may want to dispute a search result and as such reconnect with the organization that enlisted them on our directories. When this happens, the user provides additional personal information including his primary and secondary phone numbers. This information is shared directly with the organization(s) that blacklisted the user.

Why Identi Collects Information

As a public directory, we collect information to enable the community become aware of individuals or organizations that are reputable or notorious for being unavailable or unreachable after entering into deals. This information, we believe, will help them to decide whether to enter new deals with the user and also help the user to get whitelisted.

How You Can Delete Your Information

We have provided a Dispute action to enable blacklisted users on the journey of deleting their information from our directory. Using the Dispute action reconnects them with all the organizations that blacklisted them. Follow these steps to delete your information on Identi: Search for your information here.

If your name is listed, click the Dispute button to initiate a dispute. A link will be sent to your email address. Go to your email, tap on the link. You will be redirected to a form to provide information that will be used to reconnect you to the organizations that blacklisted you. Upon submitting the form, the organizations will be notified of your intentions to reconnect and will get across to you. The organizations will whitelist you once their qualm with you has been settled.

If you have taken these steps but have not been whitelisted, please feel free to contact us.
